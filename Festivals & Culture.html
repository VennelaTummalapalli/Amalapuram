Festivals & Culture

Sankranthi Celebrations

Biggest festival with rangoli, haridasu, and traditional food.

Dussehra (Vijaya Dashami)

Celebrated as the victory of good over evil with temple poojas, cultural programs, and community gatherings.

Ugadi

Telugu New Year celebrated with special poojas, Ugadi pachadi, and new beginnings in homes and temples.
