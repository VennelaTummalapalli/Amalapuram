Famous Temples Amalapuram

Venkateswara Swamy Temple

is a popular Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu (Venkateswara/Balaji) and known for affordable marriages and special Saturday prasadam.

Sri Devi Ammavaru

is a significant local Hindu shrine in Amalapuram known for its vibrant Navratri celebrations and grand decorations for the goddess.

Sri Chenna Malleswara Swamy Temple

is an ancient, wish-fulfilling Lord Shiva shrine located in Mobarlipet, primarily visited during Mahashivaratri for spiritual blessings.

Sri Subbalamma Temple

a prominent Hindu shrine located in Amalapuram, Andhra Pradesh. It is dedicated to the local deity Sri Subbalamma Thalli, who is revered as the Grama Devatha (village protector/deity) of Amalapuram.

Sri Vasavi Kanyaka Parameshwari Temple

The main temple is in Penugonda (West Godavari District), while Amalapuram has associated institutions like function halls and shops.
